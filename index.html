FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you come out?

We offer same-day service for most repairs. If you call before noon, we'll typically be there the same day. For urgent issues, just let us know and we'll prioritize your call.

What does the service call include?

Our service call fee covers the diagnosis. We'll identify the problem, explain what's needed, and give you an exact price. You decide if you want to proceed - no obligation.

Are your repairs guaranteed?

Absolutely. All our repairs come with a 90-day parts and labor warranty. If the same issue comes back within 90 days, we'll fix it at no charge.

What brands do you service?

We service all major brands including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Maytag, Kenmore, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, Amana, Miele, Viking, Sub-Zero, Wolf, Thermador, JennAir, and more.

Do you offer emergency service?

Yes! We understand some issues can't wait - like a refrigerator breaking down. Contact us and we'll do our best to fit you in quickly.

What are your hours?

Monday - Saturday: 8 AM - 6 PM
Sunday: By appointment

How much does a typical repair cost?

Repair costs vary depending on the issue and parts needed. We'll always provide a detailed quote before starting work.

Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?

We use quality OEM and aftermarket parts, all backed by our 90-day warranty. We'll discuss your options and help you choose what works best for your budget.
