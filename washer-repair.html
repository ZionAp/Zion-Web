Maintenance Tips

Simple steps to keep your washer running smoothly and extend its life.

  • Clean the drum monthly - Run a hot water cycle with vinegar or a washing machine cleaner.
  • Check the drain filter - Clean the filter every few months to prevent drainage issues.
  • Leave the door open - Let the drum air out between loads to prevent mold and mildew.
  • Use the right detergent - Use HE detergent in the correct amount to avoid buildup.
  • Inspect hoses yearly - Check water inlet hoses for cracks or leaks and replace every 5 years.
