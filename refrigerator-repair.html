Maintenance Tips

Simple steps to keep your refrigerator running efficiently.

  • Clean condenser coils - Vacuum or brush every 6-12 months to maintain efficiency.
  • Check door seals - Test with the dollar bill test - if it pulls out easily, replace the gasket.
  • Keep coils away from wall - Maintain at least 1 inch of space for proper airflow.
  • Set correct temperature - Keep refrigerator at 35-38°F and freezer at 0°F.
  • Clean the drip pan - Check and clean periodically to prevent odors.
