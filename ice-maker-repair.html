Maintenance Tips

Simple steps to keep your ice maker producing ice reliably.

  • Check the water supply - Ensure the water line is connected and not kinked.
  • Clean the water filter - Replace every 6 months for best ice quality.
  • Clean the ice bin - Empty and clean the bin periodically.
  • Check the temperature - Keep the freezer at 0°F for proper ice production.
  • Inspect the fill valve - Ensure it's filling the ice tray properly.
