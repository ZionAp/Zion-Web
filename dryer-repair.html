Maintenance Tips

Simple steps to keep your dryer running efficiently and safely.

  • Clean the lint trap after every load - This is the most important maintenance task.
  • Check the exhaust vent - Inspect yearly and clean out lint buildup to prevent fire hazards.
  • Keep the dryer level - Ensure it's level and at least 6 inches from the wall for proper airflow.
  • Inspect the drum seal - Check for damage that could let heat escape.
  • Clean the moisture sensors - Wipe the sensors with a cotton ball to keep drying accurate.
